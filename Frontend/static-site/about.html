About Noorul Haq

Learn about our mosque and community

Our Mission

Noorul Haq (UDS-GMSA) is dedicated to serving the Muslim community through worship, education, and social services. We strive to create an inclusive environment where Muslims can practice their faith, learn about Islam, and contribute to society.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of Islamic knowledge and practice, fostering unity, compassion, and excellence in our community while promoting understanding and cooperation with people of all faiths.

Our Values

  • Faith and devotion to Allah
  • Knowledge and continuous learning
  • Community service and charity
  • Unity and brotherhood
  • Respect and tolerance
  • Excellence in all endeavors

Our History

Founded at the University for Development Studies (UDS), the Ghana Muslim Students Association (GMSA) has been serving the student community for years. Our mosque, Noorul Haq, meaning "Light of Truth," has become a spiritual home for students and community members alike.
